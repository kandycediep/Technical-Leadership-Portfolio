## Reflection: Navigator Session

### 1. Harder Role

Before the session, I predicted that the Navigator role would be harder. After the session, that was true because it was challenging to guide my partner without taking over the keyboard. However, it became easier once I focused on giving clear directions and allowing my partner time to think.

### 2. Anti-Pattern

Before the session, I identified micro-managing as the anti-pattern I was most likely to fall into. It didn't show up as much as I thought it would because the pair programming felt more collaborative rather than giving instructions.

### 3. 5-Second Rule

My strategy for the 5-second rule was to pause before immediately correcting mistakes. The first time I noticed my Driver making an error, I waited and gave them a chance to recognize it on their own.

### 4. One Thing About My Partner

One thing I want to remember about my partner is how persistent they were when solving problems. They stayed positive when things didn't work right away and were willing to try different approaches until we found a solution.
