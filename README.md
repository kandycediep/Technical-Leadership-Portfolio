### Why Technical Leadership?

Technical communication is critical when working collaboratively in a team where effectively explaining what you built in a simple way enables a dynamic workflow where feedback and questions flourish. Leadership especially is important in the world of tech because being successful in this field requires not only on the ability to write code, but also taking initiative to lead teams and communicating clearly.
